About

I grew up in the mountains with a passion for skiing and the outdoors, but at the same time I made it a habit to practice taking apart computers and electronics and putting them back together. Slowly tech became a new passion alongside my life long dream of being a ski bum. I got live that dream for a long time until COVID changed everything. I went back to school graduated with my degree in IT and started learning to program. I also helped a couple of friends with their websites, and a new interest of mine was born. I have been avidly coding and programming for just under a year now and have become proficient in full-stack web development and software engineering. My front end languages include: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React.js, and ejs. My backend languages are Node.js and MongoDB as well as some SQL too. I am always adding new frameworks and languages to my repetoir and am an avid learner and thrive on perfection.
